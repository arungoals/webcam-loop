30-Second Looping Webcam Recorder

Instructions
  • **Important:** For best experience on mobile, please use the app in **horizontal (landscape)** orientation.
  • Click "Start Recording" to begin a series of 30-second recordings.
  • The **live video preview** will appear as a small thumbnail.
  • Each 30-second clip will automatically replace the "Last Recorded Video" below, which will be a larger view.
  • Click "Stop Recording" to end the continuous recording cycle.
  • Click the 'Flip Camera' button to switch between front and back cameras (on mobile devices).
  • Use the **zoom slider** below the recorded video to zoom in/out on the **last recorded video**.
  • Playing the "Last Recorded Video" will automatically stop any ongoing recording.
  • The screen will automatically stay on while recording is active to prevent interruptions.
